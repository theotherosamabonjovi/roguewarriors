📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Eliminate all enemy units, or complete the game-mode objective (CTF, Bomb, VIP Escort). Each Warrior has 1 Heart (HP). Lose all Hearts = eliminated.

⚡ Turn Structure — Alternate Activations

  • Players alternate activating one unit at a time.
  • Each activated unit gets 2 Actions.
  • Once all units have activated, a new Round begins. Pinned status resolves at start of activation.

👣 Actions

  • Move (1 action): Move up to your MA (Movement Allowance = 5" for most warriors). Click a blue tile.
  • Run (1 action): After moving, roll 1D6 for extra tiles. Cannot shoot this activation.
  • Shoot (1 action): Fire at an enemy in range & line of sight. Hits on 4+. Click a red enemy.
  • Aim (1 action): +1 to next Shooting Attack roll (effectively hits on 3+).
  • Light Cover (1 action): −1 to incoming attack rolls until next activation.
  • Heavy Cover (1 action): −2 to incoming attack rolls on a cover tile.
  • Charge (2 actions): Move into base contact and make a melee attack.
  • Reload (1 action): Clear a Weapon Jam or reload a Sniper Rifle.

🎲 Shooting Attack Sequence

  • Roll the weapon's Attack Dice. Each die hits on 4+.
  • Modifiers: −1 Light Cover (need 5+) · −2 Heavy Cover (need 6+) · +1 Aim or half-range (need 3+) · SMG: −1 to all rolls (need 5+).
  • For each Hit: target rolls 1D6 Armour Save + weapon AP modifier:
  • 🟥 1–2: 1 Damage (lose 1 Heart) · 🟪 3–4: Pinned (can't move next activation) · 🟩 5–6: Saved.

🔫 Weapons

  • Pistol/Revolver: 6", 2D6, AP 0. Dual-wield = 4D6.
  • Shotgun: 8", 3D6, AP −1. Hit = knock target back 1".
  • Submachine Gun: 12", 5D6, AP 0. −1 to Shooting Attack rolls.
  • Assault Rifle: 18", 3D6, AP 0. Three 6s = bonus AP −1. Three 1s = Weapon Jam.
  • Light Machine Gun: 3–24", 4D6, AP 0. Natural 6 = Auto-Pin. −1 MA. Scout cannot use.
  • Sniper Rifle: 6–36", 1D6, AP −1. Hit = 1D3 bonus damage. Must Reload after each shot.
  • Frag Grenade: Thrown 6". Hits everyone within 3" (ignores cover). AP −2. Roll of 1 = dud check.

🦸 Specialists

  • Rifleman: Assault Rifle. Reliable at all ranges. Cheap backbone unit.
  • Sniper: Sniper Rifle. Long-range damage dealer. Overwatch ability.
  • Medic: SMG. Heal ability — revive or un-pin an adjacent ally once per game.
  • Grenadier: Frag Grenade. Blast hits everyone in 3" AoE, ignores cover.
  • Leader: Assault Rifle. Command Aura — allies within 3 tiles reroll one failed attack die.
  • Scout: SMG, MA 6". Stealth — enemies need natural 6 to hit while in cover.
